Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fruit Merge Online handles information when you visit and use the website.

Information You Provide

Fruit Merge Online does not require you to create an account or provide your name, address, payment information, or other personal information in order to play the game.

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the information included in your message. We use that information only to respond to your enquiry.

Local Browser Storage

The game may use local browser storage to save information such as your best score, recent scores, music preference, and other game settings.

This information is normally stored on your own device. You can remove it by clearing your browser data or local website storage.

Technical Information

Website hosting, domain, security, or infrastructure providers may process standard technical information needed to deliver and protect the website. This may include IP addresses, browser information, request times, device information, and server logs.

Cookies

Fruit Merge Online does not currently require advertising or account cookies to play the game. Browser storage may still be used for game scores and preferences.

Third-Party Services

The website may rely on third-party hosting, domain, security, or infrastructure services. These providers may process information under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Fruit Merge Online does not knowingly request personal information from children. Parents and guardians should supervise children's internet use where appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the website, game features, or data practices change. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be submitted through our contact page.
