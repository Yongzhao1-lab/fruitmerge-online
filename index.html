GAME FEATURES

Why Play Fruit Merge Online?

Instant Browser Play

Start immediately on desktop or mobile. No account, download, or installation is required.

See the next three fruits and plan several moves ahead instead of reacting to only one drop.

Local Score Records

Your best score and recent runs are stored locally in your browser so you can challenge your own record.

HOW TO PLAY

How to Play Fruit Merge Online

1. Choose a Drop Position

Move your mouse on desktop or tap the desired horizontal position on mobile.

2. Merge Matching Fruits

When two identical fruits touch, they combine into the next fruit and add points to your score.

3. Stay Below the Danger Line

Keep the stack low. The run ends when settled fruits remain above the danger line for too long.

STRATEGY

Fruit Merge Tips for a Higher Score

Build a Low Base

Keep larger fruits low and avoid creating narrow towers that can quickly cross the danger line.

Protect the Center Lane

A clear center gives you more control over future drops and helps connect fruits on either side.

Plan with the Queue

Use all three previewed fruits to prepare pairs, chain merges, and safe temporary placements.
