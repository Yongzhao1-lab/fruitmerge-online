HOW TO PLAY

How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Drop Fruits

Move your cursor or finger to choose where the next fruit will fall.

Merge Matching Fruits

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a bigger fruit and increase your score.

Avoid Overflow

Keep fruits below the danger line to survive longer and score higher.

TIPS

Fruit Merge Tips

Use the Corners

Keep bigger fruits near the corners to build stable merge chains.

Watch the Next Fruit

Plan your drop position by checking the next fruit preview.

Keep the Center Open

A clean center gives new fruits more room to fall and merge naturally.
