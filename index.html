Guide

How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Fruit Merge Online is a casual puzzle game where you drop fruits into a box. When two matching fruits touch, they merge into a bigger fruit. Your goal is to keep the board under control while creating larger fruits and building a higher score.

1

Aim

Move the current fruit left or right before dropping it.

2

Drop

Click or tap to drop the fruit into the box.

3

Merge

When two matching fruits touch, they become a bigger fruit.

4

Survive

Keep fruits below the danger line for as long as possible.

Strategy

Fruit Merge Tips

Plan Before Dropping

Do not drop fruits too quickly. A small pause can help you choose a better position.

Keep Big Fruits Low

Large fruits take up more space. Try to keep them near the bottom of the box.

Control the Center

The center area is important. If it gets blocked too early, merging becomes harder.

Progression

Fruit Levels
