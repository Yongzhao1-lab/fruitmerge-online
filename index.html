How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Fruit Merge Online is a casual puzzle game where you drop fruits into a box. When two matching fruits touch, they merge into a bigger fruit. The challenge is to keep the board under control while creating larger fruits and building a higher score.

  • Move the current fruit left or right.
  • Click or tap to drop the fruit.
  • Merge two matching fruits to create a bigger fruit.
  • Keep fruits below the danger line.
  • The game ends when fruits stay near the top for too long.

Fruit Merge Tips

A good strategy is to keep similar fruits close together, avoid random drops, and build from smaller fruits to larger fruits in a controlled area.

Plan Before Dropping

Do not drop fruits too quickly. A small pause can help you choose a better position.

Keep Big Fruits Low

Large fruits take up more space. Try to keep them near the bottom of the box.

Control the Center

The center area is important. If it gets blocked too early, merging becomes harder.

Fruit Levels

Merge fruits step by step to unlock larger fruits. Watermelon is not the final fruit in this version, so keep merging to discover higher levels.
