How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Fruit Merge Online is a browser puzzle game where you drop fruits into a box. When two matching fruits touch each other, they merge into a bigger fruit. Your goal is to keep merging fruits and score as high as possible before the fruits reach the danger line.

  • Move the fruit left or right.
  • Click or tap to drop the fruit.
  • Merge two matching fruits to create a bigger fruit.
  • Keep fruits below the danger line.
  • The game ends when fruits stay near the top for too long.
