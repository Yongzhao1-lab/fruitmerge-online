HOW TO PLAY

How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Drop Fruits

Move your cursor or finger to choose where the next fruit will fall.

Merge Matching Fruits

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a larger fruit and increase your score.

Avoid Overflow

Keep fruits below the danger line. If fruits stay too high for too long, the game ends.

STRATEGY

Fruit Merge Strategy Guide

Build from the Corners

Place larger fruits near the edges to create a stable base and reduce random rolling.

Plan with Next Fruits

Use the next fruit preview to prepare merges instead of dropping fruits randomly.

Keep the Center Open

A clean center gives fruits room to fall, settle, and merge naturally.
