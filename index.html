How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Fruit Merge Online is a simple puzzle game where you drop fruits into the box. When two matching fruits touch each other, they merge into a bigger fruit. Keep merging fruits and try to get the highest score before the box fills up.

  • Move the fruit left or right.
  • Drop the fruit into the box.
  • Merge two same fruits to create a bigger fruit.
  • Keep the box from filling up.
