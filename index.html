HOW TO PLAY

How to Play Fruit Merge Online

Drop Fruits

Move your cursor or finger to choose where the next fruit will fall.

Merge Matching Fruits

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a larger fruit and increase your score.

Avoid Overflow

Keep fruits below the danger line. If fruits stay too high for too long, the game ends.

TIPS

Fruit Merge Tips

Use the Corners

Place larger fruits near the corners to build a stable base and reduce random rolling.

Watch the Next Fruits

The next fruit preview helps you plan your placement before dropping.

Keep the Center Open

A clean center gives new fruits more space to fall and merge naturally.
