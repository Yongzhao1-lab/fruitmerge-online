HOW TO PLAY

How to Play Fruit Merge Online

1. Drop Fruits

Move your cursor or finger to choose where the next fruit will fall into the box.

2. Merge Matching Fruits

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into the next larger fruit and increase your score.

3. Avoid Overflow

Keep the fruits below the danger line. If fruits stay too high for too long, the round ends.

MERGE TIPS

Fruit Merge Tips

Build from the Corners

Use the sides of the glass box to organize bigger fruits and prevent random rolling.

Watch the Next Fruits

The next fruit preview helps you plan your placement before dropping.

Keep the Center Clean

A clear center gives new fruits more space to fall and merge naturally.
