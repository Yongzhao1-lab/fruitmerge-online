About Fruit Merge Online

Fruit Merge Online is a free browser-based puzzle game where players drop fruits, combine matching fruits, unlock larger fruits, and try to achieve a new high score.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a simple, relaxing, and enjoyable casual game that can be played directly in a web browser without installation, registration, or payment.

How the Game Works

Drop fruits into the game area and place matching fruits together. When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a larger fruit and add points to your score.

The challenge is to plan each drop carefully and prevent the fruits from remaining above the danger line.

Play Anywhere

Fruit Merge Online is designed for desktop computers, tablets, and mobile devices. Your best score and game preferences may be stored locally in your browser.
